How It Works

1

Apply for the Program

Complete the application form below with information about your team, your goals, and why you'd like to participate in the case study program. We're looking for teams of all skill levels who are committed to player development.

2

Selection Process

Our team will review all applications and select a diverse group of teams to participate in the program. We'll notify all applicants within two weeks of submission, and selected teams will receive immediate access to the platform.

3

Implementation & Training

Once selected, you'll receive a comprehensive onboarding session to help you implement the XYDO ATHLETIC PROGRAMS system with your team. We'll provide guidance on how to integrate the platform into your existing training schedule.

4

Regular Check-ins

Throughout the case study period (typically 8-12 weeks), we'll conduct regular check-ins to gather feedback, answer questions, and ensure you're getting the most out of the platform. These brief sessions help us understand your experience.

5

Data Collection

We'll collect data on your team's usage, progress, and results throughout the program. This includes player engagement metrics, skill development progress, and feedback from coaches and players.

6

Case Study Publication

At the end of the program, we'll compile the results into a case study that highlights your team's experience and outcomes. You'll have the opportunity to review and approve the case study before publication.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the case study program last?

The typical case study program lasts 8-12 weeks, which gives us enough time to collect meaningful data on your team's progress. However, we can be flexible based on your season schedule and specific needs.

What kind of teams are you looking for?

We're looking for a diverse range of teams across different age groups, skill levels, and geographic locations. The most important factor is a commitment to player development and willingness to provide regular feedback on the platform.

What are the expectations for participating teams?

Participating teams are expected to implement the XYDO program with their players, provide regular feedback through brief surveys and check-ins, and share their results at the end of the program. We also ask that coaches track player progress using the platform's tools.

How will our team's information be used?

We'll use your team's experience and results to create a case study that demonstrates the effectiveness of the XYDO platform. This may include before/after statistics, testimonials, and descriptions of how you implemented the program. You'll have the opportunity to review and approve all content before publication.

What happens after the case study period ends?

After the case study period, participating teams will receive a special discount to continue using the platform if they choose to do so. We'll also provide a comprehensive report of your team's progress and recommendations for continued development.

How many teams will be selected for the program?

We select a limited number of teams for each case study cohort to ensure we can provide adequate support and attention to each participating team. Typically, we accept 5-10 teams per cohort, with new cohorts starting quarterly.
