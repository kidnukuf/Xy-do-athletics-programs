How It Works

1

Complete the application form below with information about your coaching experience, social media presence, and why you'd like to become a XYDO ambassador.

Our team will review your application and reach out to discuss the partnership. We're looking for passionate advocates who believe in player development and have influence in the basketball community.

As you refer more teams and engage with the community, you'll advance through our ambassador levels, unlocking higher commission rates and exclusive benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn commissions as an ambassador?

You earn commissions when teams or organizations sign up for XYDO using your unique referral link or code. Commission rates range from 10% to 25% based on your ambassador level. Commissions are calculated on the initial subscription fee and recurring payments for as long as the referred team remains active.

When and how are commissions paid?

Commissions are paid monthly for all qualifying referrals from the previous month. Payments are made via direct deposit, PayPal, or Venmo, depending on your preference. You must reach a minimum of $50 in commissions before a payment is issued.

What qualifies as a successful referral?

A successful referral occurs when a team or organization signs up for a paid XYDO subscription using your unique referral link or code. The referral must be a new customer who has not previously had a paid XYDO account. Free trial sign-ups do not qualify until they convert to a paid subscription.

How long do I receive commissions for my referrals?

You receive commissions for the lifetime of the referred account, as long as they maintain an active paid subscription and you remain an active ambassador. This creates a recurring income stream that grows with each new referral.

What are the requirements to maintain ambassador status?

To maintain your ambassador status, you need to meet the minimum requirements for your current level, which typically includes regular social media sharing, participation in ambassador calls or webinars, and maintaining ethical promotional practices. If you're inactive for more than 6 months, your status may be reviewed.

Can I be an ambassador if I'm not a coach?

Yes! While many of our ambassadors are coaches, we also welcome athletic directors, skills trainers, former players, basketball influencers, and others who are connected to the basketball community. The most important qualification is your ability to authentically promote XYDO to potential users.
