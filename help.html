Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a team?

To join a team, you need to receive a team code from your coach or team administrator. During registration, you'll be prompted to enter this code, which will automatically connect you to your team. If you've already registered, you can add a team code in your profile settings.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically tracked as you complete training modules and activities. You can view your progress on your dashboard, which shows completion percentages, skill development metrics, and earned achievements. For more detailed analytics, visit your profile page and click on the "Progress" tab.

Can I access content offline?

Currently, most content requires an internet connection to access. However, you can download practice plans and training guides as PDFs for offline use. We're working on an offline mode for the mobile app that will allow you to download videos for offline viewing in a future update.

How do I provide feedback on training content?

Each training module includes a feedback section where you can rate the content and provide written comments. Your feedback is valuable and helps us improve the platform. Coaches can view anonymous ratings and identified written feedback to adjust training plans accordingly.

What devices can I use to access the platform?

XYDO ATHLETIC PROGRAMS is accessible on any device with a web browser, including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge browsers. We also have dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android devices that provide enhanced features for on-the-go training.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. For security reasons, the link expires after 24 hours. If you're still having trouble, please contact support.

Can coaches customize the training program?

Yes, coaches with admin privileges can customize the training program for their team. From the admin dashboard, coaches can adjust the content schedule, add custom drills and exercises, and tailor the program to their team's specific needs. Changes made by coaches will only affect their team's view of the platform.

How do I become an ambassador for XYDO?

To become an ambassador, visit the Ambassador Program page and fill out the application form. We look for coaches, players, and athletic directors who are passionate about basketball development and have an engaged community. Ambassadors receive special benefits including commission on referrals, exclusive content, and early access to new features.

Troubleshooting

Videos won't play or are buffering constantly

Solution: Try the following steps:

  1. Check your internet connection speed (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
  2. Lower the video quality in the player settings
  3. Close other applications or browser tabs that might be using bandwidth
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  5. Try a different browser or device
If the problem persists, please contact support with details about your device and internet connection.

Can't log in to my account

Solution: Try the following steps:

  1. Double-check that you're using the correct email address and password
  2. Make sure Caps Lock is turned off
  3. Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  5. Try a different browser or device
If you still can't log in, contact support with your account email address.

Progress isn't being tracked correctly

Solution: Try the following steps:

  1. Make sure you're completing activities fully (watching videos to the end, submitting all required feedback)
  2. Check that you're logged in with the correct account
  3. Refresh your browser or app
  4. Log out and log back in
  5. Wait 15-30 minutes for progress to sync across devices
If your progress still isn't tracking correctly, please contact support with specific examples of the affected activities.

Team code isn't working

Solution: Try the following steps:

  1. Double-check that you're entering the code exactly as provided (codes are case-sensitive)
  2. Make sure the code hasn't expired (team codes typically expire after 7 days)
  3. Confirm with your coach or administrator that they've generated a valid code
  4. Check that you haven't already joined the team with a different account
If the code still doesn't work, ask your coach or administrator to generate a new code.

App crashes or freezes

Solution: Try the following steps:

  1. Close and reopen the app or browser
  2. Update to the latest version of the app or browser
  3. Restart your device
  4. Check that your device meets the minimum system requirements
  5. Uninstall and reinstall the app (mobile users)
If the problem persists, please contact support with details about your device, operating system, and when the crashes occur.

Payment or billing issues

Solution: For payment or billing issues:

  1. Check that your payment method is valid and hasn't expired
  2. Verify that your billing address matches your card information
  3. Check your email for any payment notifications or receipts
  4. Review your subscription status in the Settings > Billing section
For specific billing questions or to report unauthorized charges, please contact our billing department at billing@xydoathletic.com.
