Practice Plan

  • 1

    Warm-up: Dynamic Ball Handling

    Complete a series of dynamic ball handling drills while moving at game speed.

    • Two-ball dribbling sequence (2 minutes)
    • Figure-8 dribbling (2 minutes)
    • Crossover series at half-court (3 minutes)
    7 min
  • 2

    Decision Making Drill: Read and React

    Practice making quick decisions based on defensive positioning.

    • Set up cones to represent defenders
    • Dribble at each cone and make a move based on its position
    • Finish with a pass or shot depending on the situation
    10 min
  • 3

    Pressure Handling: 1-on-1 Full Court

    Work with a partner to simulate full-court pressure defense.

    • One player dribbles full court against defensive pressure
    • Focus on maintaining control and making good decisions
    • Switch roles after each repetition
    15 min
  • 4

    Game Simulation: 3-on-3 Ball Screen Actions

    Apply ball handling skills in 3-on-3 situations with ball screens.

    • Set up 3-on-3 half-court scenarios
    • Ball handler must use screen and make appropriate decision
    • Focus on reading the defense and making the right play
    20 min
  • 5

    Cool Down: Ball Handling Visualization

    Mentally rehearse game situations while performing light ball handling.

    • Visualize game scenarios while dribbling
    • Practice decision-making through mental imagery
    • Focus on breathing and recovery
    5 min

Mental Training

Decision-Making Visualization

This exercise helps you improve your decision-making by mentally rehearsing game situations.

Find a quiet space and sit comfortably. Close your eyes and visualize yourself in a game situation with the ball. See yourself reading the defense, making decisions, and executing the right play. Imagine different defensive scenarios and practice making quick decisions in your mind.

Spend 10-15 minutes on this exercise, focusing on clarity and detail in your visualization.
