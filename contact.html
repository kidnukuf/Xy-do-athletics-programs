Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about XYDO ATHLETIC PROGRAMS.

How do I sign up for XYDO ATHLETIC PROGRAMS?

Signing up for XYDO is easy! You can register as a player or coach through our registration page. To join a specific team, you'll need a team code from your head coach or administrator. If you're a head coach or administrator looking to set up a new team, please contact us directly for assistance.

What devices can I use to access XYDO?

XYDO ATHLETIC PROGRAMS is accessible on any device with an internet connection, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. Our responsive design ensures a great experience regardless of screen size.

How much does XYDO cost?

XYDO offers flexible pricing options for teams and organizations. We also have a free tier with limited features and a case study program that provides full access in exchange for feedback. Contact us for specific pricing information based on your team's size and needs.

Can I use XYDO for sports other than basketball?

Currently, XYDO is focused on basketball, but we're actively developing programs for additional sports. Our platform is designed with a flexible architecture that will accommodate multiple sports in the future. Contact us if you're interested in being notified when new sports are added.

How do I become an ambassador for XYDO?

We're always looking for passionate coaches and athletes to join our ambassador program! Visit our Ambassador Program page to learn about the benefits and application process. Ambassadors receive commissions for referrals, exclusive content, and other perks.
